Pico De Gallo

Description

A fresh salsa that goes well with many dishes and isn't too spicy, so everyone can enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 3 Roma tomatoes
  • 1 small white onion
  • 1 large jalapeno
  • 1 lime
  • 1 bunch of cilantro
  • 1 Tsp salt

Steps

  1. Finely dice tomatoes, onion, and jalapeno and add to a bowl
  2. Chop up cilantro finely and add to taste
  3. Squeeze juice from lime into bowl
  4. Add salt to taste and stir to incorporate all ingredients
  5. Serve with tortilla chips, nachos, enchiladas, or anything really
