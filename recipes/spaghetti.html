Spicy Spaghetti

Description

Spaghetti with Italian Sausage and a little bit of spice to it!

Ingredients

  • 2 28oz cans of peeled San Marzano tomatoes
  • 1 pound of spaghetti noodles
  • 1 8oz can of tomato paste
  • 1 large yellow onion
  • 1 bulb of garlic
  • 5 Italian sausages
  • 3 Tbsp oregano
  • 3 Tbsp red pepper flakes
  • 4 Tbsp butter
  • 3 Tbsp olive oil

Steps

  1. In a large pot, heat olive oil on medium heat until shimmering, approximately 5 minutes
  2. While oil is heating up, dice the onion and mince the garlic
  3. Once oil is shimmering, add sausage to the pot and cook for 5 minutes, then flip and cook for 5 minutes on the other side. The sausage will not be fully cooked at this point, but that is ok, we will finish in later steps
  4. Remove sausage from pot, put in on a plate in the refrigerator to stop cooking
  5. Add minced garlic and constantly stir until fragrant, approximately one minute
  6. Add diced onion and stir occasionally until onion starts to turn translucent, approximately 5 minutes
  7. Add tomato paste, oregano, and red pepper flakes, stir to incorporate and cook an additional 5 minutes
  8. Empty the cans of San Marzano tomatoes into a bowl and crush them with your hands until they are small tomato chunks, then add the bowl of tomatoes to the pot with the other ingredients
  9. Take the sausages out of the fridge and chop them into bit size chunks. Add the chopped sausage and any of their juices to the sauce, stirring everything to incorporate
  10. Reduce heat until the sauce is just simmering. Cook for 1 to 2 hours
  11. Once the sauce has been cooking for at least 1 hour, boil some water in a separate pot and add the spaghetti, cooking til al dente, approximately 8 minutes
  12. Drain spaghetti and add noodles and butter to the sauce, stirring everything together
  13. Remove from heat and let sit for a few minutes to cool
  14. Serve spaghetti with parmesan cheese and garlic bread, enjoy!
